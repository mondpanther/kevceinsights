EMDE R&D Lab Tracker

Research, design and engineering labs that the world's leading R&D-investing firms (EU R&D Scoreboard) run in emerging markets and developing economies (EMDEs) — including firms headquartered in developing countries themselves (Huawei, BYD, Alibaba, TCS, Embraer, Reliance Jio …). Candidate firms were screened two ways — by their developing-country patent activity and by their global R&D budget (the latter catches big R&D spenders that patent little, e.g. Maruti Suzuki, Ubisoft, NetEase). Solid dots are named labs verified from public sources (hover for what they do, click for sources). Hollow dots are the handful of remaining patent-flagged firms not yet individually verified.

Method: every firm in the EU Industrial R&D Investment Scoreboard is screened for a developing-country R&D lab, prioritised first by developing-country patent activity (firm_country_indicators.parquet) and then by global R&D budget, and each is checked once. Verified labs are hand-curated and source-backed, placed at their real city (co-located labs are spread slightly to avoid overlap). The few remaining patent-flagged firms are placed at a random land point within their dominant inventor country (country-level, not exact). Size figures mix scientist and engineering headcounts and are not comparable across labs.
