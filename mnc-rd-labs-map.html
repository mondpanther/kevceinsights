EMDE R&D Lab Tracker

Each dot is a research, design or engineering centre that a high-income-country multinational operates in an emerging market or developing economy (EMDE). Hover a dot for what the lab does; click it for sources and the parent firm's developing-country innovation share from the EU R&D Scoreboard panel. The point of the map: dispersed corporate R&D is not just a China-and-India story — most of these labs are elsewhere.
