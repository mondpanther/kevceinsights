EMDE R&D Lab Tracker — an interactive world map
June 2026 · Ralf

673 verified R&D labs that the world's leading R&D-investing firms run in emerging and developing economies — including developing-country champions like Huawei, BYD, Alibaba, TCS, Embraer, Arçelik, Gree, Hikvision, SMIC and Sinopec. Two pipelines feed the map (toggle between them): firms from the EU R&D Scoreboard, and a new patent-holder pipeline that ranks the owners of developing-country (ex-China/India) innovation — surfacing domestic owners the scoreboard misses, such as Turkcell, Ford-Otosan, Petrobras/CENPES, CONICET, MIMOS and leading Brazilian, Mexican and South African universities. Hover any dot for what the lab does. The point: frontier corporate R&D reaches far beyond a few rich-world hubs.
